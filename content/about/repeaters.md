# Iowa Radio Operators (IRO) Repeaters

K0IRO currently operates two repeaters at our primary site in Mingo, IA.

### **VHF**
**Frequency:** 146.670 / 146.070  
**PL:** 114.8 DL / 114.8 UL  
**AllStar Node:** 656830  
**Hams Over IP Trunk:** 15098  

RepeaterBook listing:  
https://www.repeaterbook.com/repeaters/details.php?state_id=19&ID=39

### **UHF**
**Frequency:** 442.425 / 447.425  
**PL:** 151.4 DL / 151.4 UL  
**DMR:** Color Code 2  

For best performance, program the PL tone on both transmit and receive; otherwise, you may hear DMR bursts when DMR is active.

RepeaterBook listing:  
https://www.repeaterbook.com/repeaters/details.php?state_id=19&ID=24023
