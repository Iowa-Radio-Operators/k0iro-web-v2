# Local Nets Around Iowa

Across Iowa, amateur radio operators gather on local nets each week to share information, practice good operating habits, and strengthen our community. These nets are more than just check‑ins — they provide a vital opportunity to test equipment, stay familiar with emergency procedures, and connect with fellow hams across the state. Regular participation helps ensure that when communication really matters, we’re ready.

If you’d like to have your net added to this list, please email **community@k0iro.com** with the details.
