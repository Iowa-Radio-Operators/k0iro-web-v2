# A Fresh Look for Iowa Radio Operators

If you’ve stopped by recently, you’ve probably noticed things look a little different. We’ve rolled out a new layout and navigation structure designed to make the site faster, clearer, and easier to use. Whether you’re checking event dates, submitting logs, or exploring what’s happening across Iowa’s amateur radio community, everything should now feel more intuitive.

We also want to acknowledge that things have been a bit quiet on the website. Like many of you, we’ve been juggling several projects behind the scenes, and a few of them pulled us away longer than expected. Thank you for your patience — and keep an eye out, because we have another announcement coming soon about what’s been keeping us busy.

For now, we hope you enjoy the cleaner design, improved readability, and simplified menus. If you spot anything that could be improved, or if you have ideas that would make the site even more useful, we’d love your feedback.

Thanks for sticking with us, for supporting the club, and for keeping Iowa’s airwaves active.
